3)  Installing MT 7 (Touch Panel Driver)
================================================================================
1.  Log in with Administrative privileges. 
2.  Connect the USB cable to the PC USB port and to the monitor USB port before 
    installing the GenTouch (touch panel driver).
3.  Double click "Setup.exe" in [GenTouch]-[English] folder.
4.  Follow the instructions.
